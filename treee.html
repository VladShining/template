I think I have not yet spoken. many opinions. enough soul to understand them. seen two of them die. They were full of horror, but silent. bright minds express astonishment. that all of them without exception suggested escape. said that it existed. we were not alone. there is no choice. I am speaking here of difficult places to retreat. men enclined to be in harmony with themselves decency. convinced that this ignorance explains everything, and that in darkness is my light. that human existance is an utter absurdity for anyone without faith in immortality. going to introduce hope in a strange form. escape by the leap. keep to myself. I know what I cannot deny. links them. they have in common. need. are not. that the absurd cannot be settled. one can compromise. it is impossible for me to know. it implies obscurity and ignorance.
